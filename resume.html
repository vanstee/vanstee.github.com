Focus

Product development is what I'm focused on. That means I love things like working directly with users, designing and developing new features, mercilessly deleting features that go unused, continuously shipping, and always working towards product-market fit.

The sweet spot between my interests and experience is when I have the autonomy to push forward to build what customers want, with an engineering culture that promotes high-quality, mantainable code, while still tackling complex problems, both business and engineering related.

But, I also have my programming hobby interests. I've always loved reading about distrubited computing, so things like Erlang/Elixir, CRDTs and Raft have been what I've been learning about lately. Also, I enjoy building HTTP APIs, so I've been writing applications in Ember with APIs backed by Rails in my free time.
